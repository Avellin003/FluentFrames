Why Learn with Us

Our Special Features

Structured Lessons with Clear Progress

Our curriculum is carefully designed to guide learners’ step by step. Each lesson builds on the last, ensuring that grammar, vocabulary, and expressions are introduced with clarity and purpose. You will always know exactly what you are learning and how to apply it.

Real-Life Stories and Resources

We go beyond textbooks by using stories, podcasts, and transcripts that reflect everyday situations. This helps you understand how the language is naturally spoken, bridging the gap between structured study and real-world communication.

1-on-1 Practice with Native Speakers

Speaking with native speakers is the most effective way to build confidence and fluency. Our personalized sessions provide a supportive space to practice conversation, receive feedback, and refine your communication skills.

AI-Powered Learning Assistant

Your personal learning companion, available anytime. It helps you find words and phrases, practice exercises, get feedback, and guide your next steps — making learning easier, interactive, and effective.

About Us

What is Fluent Frames?

Fluent Frames is a trusted language learning platform used by students around the world to master Kinyarwanda effectively. We provide a complete learning experience that combines structured lessons, real-life stories, transcripts, and personalized guidance. Students can choose to learn online or in person, practice at their own pace, and engage with native speakers to build real conversational skills. We have supported many learners, helping them study confidently, reinforce their skills, and achieve fluency through an approach that is both practical and enjoyable.

Meet the Founder

Driven by Passion, Committed to Excellence in Education.

Hi, I’m Herve Shema. Founder of Fluent Frames. I’ve always loved learning languages, and through my own experiences, I realized how rewarding it is to study in a way that is structured, engaging, and keeps your motivation up. Inspired by this, I created Fluent Frames to make Kinyarwanda more accessible to learners everywhere. My goal is to help students gain confidence, enjoy the process, and develop fluency step by step, with tools and guidance that make learning both practical and enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our courses and learning platform.

Who are Fluent Frames courses for?

Our courses are designed for anyone wanting to learn Kinyarwanda — from complete beginners to advanced learners seeking fluency.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Yes! All lessons are structured so you can progress at your own speed. You can revisit videos, exercises, and transcripts as often as needed.

Do you offer 1-on-1 tutoring?

Absolutely! You can schedule personalized lessons with native Kinyarwanda tutors, available online or in-person, depending on your preference.

What does the AI-powered assistant do?

Our AI assistant helps you practice anytime by looking up words, providing exercises, giving feedback, and suggesting what to study next — all to reinforce your learning.

Are your transcripts and exercises included?

Depending on your membership tier, you’ll get transcripts, vocabulary lists, and interactive exercises for either free videos or all content.

How do I get started?

Simply choose a membership plan that suits your needs, sign up, and start learning right away!

Is my payment secure, and do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes! All payments are processed securely through trusted providers. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all membership plans, so you can try Fluent Frames risk-free.

Is Fluent Frames a legitimate platform?

Absolutely! Fluent Frames is a trusted language learning platform used by students worldwide. Our structured lessons, native tutors, and AI-powered tools are designed to help learners progress effectively and safely.

Can I cancel or change my membership anytime?

Yes! You have full control over your membership. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time without hassle.
